## 🎯 Business Lines & Services

**Inflow Network** is a B2B SaaS marketplace connecting:
- **Brands** (advertisers seeking influencer partnerships)
- **Content Creators** (influencers, streamers, digital trendsetters)
- **Multi-platform coverage** (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn)

### Core Services
1. **Influencer Marketplace** - Creator database discovery and matching
2. **Campaign Management** - End-to-end orchestration, approval workflows, analytics
3. **Creator Tools** - Recruitment, contract management, payment processing
4. **Performance Analytics** - Campaign tracking and ROI measurement

## 💼 Technology Stack

- **Platform**: WordPress (WPBakery, Slider Revolution)
- **Frontend**: JavaScript, jQuery, custom plugins
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Google Site Kit
- **Integrations**: Multi-platform social APIs (Meta, YouTube, TikTok, etc.)
- **Payments**: Creator compensation systems
- **Infrastructure**: Hosted WordPress infrastructure

## 🤖 AI/ML Opportunity Areas

### HIGH PRIORITY
1. **Intelligent Creator Matching**
   - Problem: Brands spend hours finding right creators for campaigns
   - Solution: ML-powered recommendations based on audience fit, engagement, brand alignment
   - Impact: Faster deal closures, higher success rates
   - Required Skills: Recommendation systems, audience segmentation

2. **Campaign Performance Prediction**
   - Problem: Brands cannot estimate ROI before launch
   - Solution: Predictive models for engagement, reach, conversion
   - Impact: Better campaign selection, reduced failures
   - Required Skills: Predictive modeling, data analysis

### MEDIUM PRIORITY

   - Automated budget allocation, creator mix adjustment
   - Impact: Higher margins, better client ROI
