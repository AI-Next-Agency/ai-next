## 🎯 Misyon & Vizyon

Fizix develops advanced machine health monitoring solutions powered by AI and IoT technology. They help businesses across multiple industries detect potential machine failures in advance, optimize maintenance processes, extend machine lifespan, and increase production efficiency.

## 💼 İş Modeli

**Core Product**: Smart sensors + AI-driven software platform
- Advanced sensors analyze machine parameters in real-time
- AI platform senses and interprets data comprehensively
- Predictive maintenance capabilities to prevent downtime
