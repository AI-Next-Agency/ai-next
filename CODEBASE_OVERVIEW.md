# Codebase Overview - AI Education & Consultancy Venture

## Project Context

**Repository**: `ai-next`  
**Purpose**: Working space for an AI education and consultancy venture  
**Partners**: Giray (G) and Nihat (N)  
**Current Focus**: Curriculum design, assessment forms, and presentation material  
**Status**: Pre-product phase (not a shipping software product yet)

---

## Vision & Mission

The venture aims to establish authority in AI through:
- **Education**: Training teams and companies on AI skills
- **Consultancy**: Providing tailored guidance and workshops
- **Long-term**: Potentially undertaking large-scale projects

**Core Value Proposition**: Help companies and teams acquire new AI-related skills needed in this rapidly changing world through personalized education and workshops.

- **Owner**: Nihat (N)

- **Deliverable**: Google Form → Google Sheets integration
- **Purpose**: Initial assessment tool to evaluate teams' current AI capabilities and skill levels

### 2. **Curriculum Design** (Priority 2)
- **Owner**: Giray (G)

  - Design personalized curriculum flow
- **Input**: Assessment form results will inform curriculum

### 3. **First Presentation** (Priority 3)
- **Purpose**: Initial pitch to companies
- **Owner**: To be confirmed
- **Expected Content**: Demonstration of value proposition and sample curriculum

### 4. **First Form Project** (`projects/first-form/`)
- **File**: `giray-questions.md`
- **Content**: Initial outreach template/questions for prospective company clients
- **Questions Cover**:
  1. Coding agents usage (Codex App, Terminal vs IDE preferences)
  2. LLM foundational knowledge level
  3. Automated LLM usage (background agents, code generation)
  4. MCP/Skill/Plugin expertise and organizational management
  5. Expectations from AI and desired skill gaps to fill
  6. General LLM industry awareness (tools, bottlenecks, trends)
